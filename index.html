Understanding Indian Cuisine: A Blend of History, Culture, and Flavor

Indian cuisine has evolved over thousands of years, shaped by diverse influences from across the world. From ancient royal kitchens to modern-day family meals, it incorporates a range of ingredients and cooking techniques that vary by region, climate, and historical influence. Spices—often referred to as the soul of Indian food—are the primary reason behind its complex flavors. These spices are used not just for flavor but for their medicinal properties, which have been passed down through generations.

At Top of India, we incorporate time-honored recipes passed down through the generations. Every dish we serve tells the story of the region it comes from, the people who created it, and the influences that made it what it is today. Whether you’re enjoying the creamy richness of a North Indian butter chicken or the spicy tang of a South Indian dosa, you are experiencing the unique legacy of one of the world’s oldest and most diverse cuisines.

The Spices that Define Indian Cuisine

Spices are the heart and soul of Indian cuisine. Indian food can be remarkably different from what many people are accustomed to, especially when compared to Western cuisine. In Indian cooking, it’s not just about heat from chilies but about the balanced and layered use of spices to bring out the best flavors in food. Among the key spices commonly used are cumin, coriander, turmeric, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and mustard seeds.

At Top of India, we take great pride in sourcing the freshest, highest-quality spices available to ensure each dish maintains its authentic flavor. Whether it’s the earthy tones of cumin or the pungent warmth of garam masala, each dish reflects the depth and complexity of Indian cuisine. Our chefs are experts in using these spices in perfect harmony, creating a flavor profile that is bold, aromatic, and undeniably delicious.

Popular Indian Dishes at Top of India

The diversity of Indian food can be overwhelming for newcomers, but it’s one of the things that make Indian cuisine so exciting. At Top of India, we offer a wide array of dishes that represent the different regions of India. Here are a few crowd favorites that showcase the true essence of our cuisine:

1. Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

A global favorite, Butter Chicken is a dish that perfectly encapsulates the rich and creamy textures that are characteristic of North Indian cuisine. Originating in Delhi, butter chicken is made with tender pieces of chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in a smooth, buttery tomato sauce. At Top of India, our butter chicken is made from scratch, with the finest ingredients ensuring a perfect balance of savory, creamy, and slightly tangy flavors.

2. Biryani

Biryani is an aromatic rice dish that originated in the Mughal kitchens and has become a staple throughout India. Made with basmati rice, meat (such as chicken, lamb, or beef), and a blend of spices, this dish is both comforting and indulgent. The secret to the perfect biryani lies in its balance of spices, the quality of the meat, and the technique of slow-cooking. At Top of India, we take special care in cooking our biryani, using traditional methods to ensure each bite is fragrant and packed with layers of flavor.

3. Dosa with Sambar and Coconut Chutney

Representing the South of India, dosa is a crispy, thin pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. Traditionally served with sambar (a spicy, tangy lentil soup) and coconut chutney, dosa is a light yet satisfying meal that can be enjoyed at any time of day. At Top of India, we make our dosa fresh to order, ensuring a perfect balance of crispy edges and soft interiors, with a variety of fillings to choose from, such as potato masala, paneer, and more.

4. Tandoori Dishes

Tandoori cooking is one of the most iconic styles of cooking in India, made using a traditional clay oven called a tandoor. Meats and vegetables are marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, then roasted in the tandoor, resulting in tender, smoky, and flavorful dishes. At Top of India, our tandoori menu includes favorites like tandoori chicken, seekh kebabs, and tandoori paneer. Each dish is perfectly charred and bursting with flavor.

5. Saag Paneer

For those seeking a vegetarian option, saag paneer is a must-try. This North Indian dish features paneer (Indian cottage cheese) simmered in a spiced spinach gravy. It’s creamy, flavorful, and comforting, making it a popular choice for both vegetarians and meat-lovers alike. We pride ourselves on preparing this dish with the finest spinach, ensuring a smooth and velvety texture that pairs perfectly with freshly made naan bread.
